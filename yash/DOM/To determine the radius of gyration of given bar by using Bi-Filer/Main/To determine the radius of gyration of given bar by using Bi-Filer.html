Dynamics Of Machine
Experiment No. 03
To determine the radius of gyration of given bar by using Bi-Filer suspension

Apparatus
Rectangular section bar, Cord, Scale, Stopwatch.


Description of Set up
A uniform rectangular section bar is suspended from the pendulum support frame by two parallel cords. Top ends of the cords pass through the two small chucks fitted at the top. Other ends are secured in the bifilar bar. It is possible to adjust the length of the cord by loosening the chuck. The suspension may also be used to determine the radius of gyration of any body. In this case the body under investigation is bolted to the centre. Radius of gyration of the combine bar and body is then determine.

Procedure
  1. Suspend the bar from chuck and adjust the length of cord ‘L’ conveniently.
  2. Note the suspension length of each cord must be the same.
  3. Allow the bar to oscillate about the vertical axis passing through the center and measure the periodic time T by knowing the time for say 10 oscillations.
  4. Repeat the experiment by mounting the weights at equal distance from the center.
  5. Complete the observation table given below.

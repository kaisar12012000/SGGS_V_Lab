Dynamics Of Machine
Experiment No. 01
To verify the relation

Introduction of Experiment :

In this experiment, we shall know about the pendulum and how to find the frequency of vibration

Apparatus :

  • Pendulum
  • Scale
  • Stopwatch
  • Thread

    • Description of Set up :

    For conducting the experiment, each ball is supported by nylon thread into the chuck. It is possible to change the length of pendulum by drawing the thread through chuck. This makes it possible to study the effect of variation of length on periodic time. A small ball may be substituted for large ball to illustrate that the period of oscillation is independent of mass of the ball.

    Physical Lab Procedure :


    1. Attach the each ball to one end of thread.

    2. Loosen the nut at the top of chuck and draw the thread to adjust the length.

    3. Allow the ball to oscillate and determine the periodic time ‘T’ by knowing the time for say 10 oscillations.

    4. Repeat the experiment by changing the length.

    5. Complete the observation table given below.

    Virtual Lab Procedure :


    1. Attach the each ball to one end of thread.

    2. Loosen the nut at the top of chuck and draw the thread to adjust the length.

    3. Allow the ball to oscillate and determine the periodic time ‘T’ by knowing the time for say 10 oscillations.

    4. Repeat the experiment by changing the length.

    5. Complete the observation table given below.
